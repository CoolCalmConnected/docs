## What is ScriptKit?

ScriptKit is an AI-powered video production platform that transforms your video scripts into production-ready images and videos. Whether you're a content creator, marketer, or filmmaker, ScriptKit helps you visualize your ideas faster than ever before.

## Why ScriptKit?

### Speed Up Your Production Workflow

Traditional video production requires extensive planning, stock footage searches, and asset creation. ScriptKit condenses this into a simple chat-based interface where you describe what you need and AI delivers.

### Consistent Visual Style

Generate multiple variations of each scene while maintaining visual consistency across your entire project. Save your favorites to the Vault for reuse across projects.
